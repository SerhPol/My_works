Hellow
The winner is the one who knocks all lives out of the opponent

There are two options for warriors:

Fighter:
  life - 150
  damage - 15
Archer:
  life - 100
  damage - 10

Control:
  first player (top right):
   w - up
   s - down
   a - left
   d - right
   space - attack

  second player (bottom left):
   (arrow control)
   up arrow - up
   down arrow - down
   left arrow - left
   right arrow - right
   enter - attack

  !! the shot is fired in that direction,
  !! in which the last step was taken
